School Intro

Matrikiran


Managed by the Auro Education Society, the educational arm of the Vatika Group, MatriKiran is an English medium, co-educational school for Grades Pre-Nursery to 12. The 8.25 acre campus is spread over two locations – the Junior School, Sohna Road, on 2 acres, and the Senior School, Sector 83, on 6.25 acres. The Junior School, (Pre-Nursery to Grade 5) MatriKiran–Sohna Road, commenced its first academic session on 4th April 2011, whereas the High School at Vatika India Next, commenced on 4th April 2016.
